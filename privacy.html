Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Last Updated: January 2024

At Nano Cropper+, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free image cropping tool. The short version: we don't collect, store, or transmit your images or personal data.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Unlike many online services, Nano Cropper+ is designed with privacy at its core. We do NOT collect:

  • Your uploaded images or photos
  • Processed or cropped image files
  • Image metadata (EXIF data, location information, etc.)
  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses (unless you contact us voluntarily)
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes
  • Browsing behavior or usage patterns

2. How Our Tool Works (Client-Side Processing)

Nano Cropper+ uses client-side processing technology, which means:

  • All Processing Happens in Your Browser: When you upload an image, it is loaded directly into your web browser's memory using HTML5 Canvas technology.
  • No Server Upload: Your images are NEVER uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers. They stay on your device throughout the entire process.
  • Temporary Memory Only: Images exist only in your browser's temporary memory (RAM) while you're actively using the tool.
  • Automatic Deletion: When you close the browser tab or navigate away from the page, all image data is immediately and permanently deleted from memory.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Nano Cropper+ uses minimal local storage for the following purposes:

  • Theme Preference: We store your dark mode / light mode preference in your browser's localStorage so the site remembers your choice on future visits. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We do not allow third-party services to set cookies on our website.

4. Third-Party Services

Nano Cropper+ uses the following third-party resources:

  • CDN Resources: We load some libraries (like Font Awesome icons and Cropper.js) from Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to improve performance. These services may log basic technical information like IP addresses in accordance with their own privacy policies.
  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking or analytics services.
  • No Advertising: We do not display advertisements or work with advertising networks.

5. Contact Form

If you choose to contact us through our contact form:

  • We will collect only the information you voluntarily provide (name, email address, and message).
  • This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.
  • We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to address your request.
  • You may request deletion of your contact information at any time by emailing us.

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your images or personal data, there is no data to be compromised. However, we still take security seriously:

  • Our website uses HTTPS encryption for secure communication.
  • We regularly update our codebase to patch any security vulnerabilities.
  • We follow industry best practices for web application security.

7. Children's Privacy

Nano Cropper+ does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Our service is designed to be used by people of all ages, and since we don't collect personal information, there are no special considerations needed for younger users. However, we recommend that children use our tool under parental supervision.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data or images, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your information because it never leaves your device.

If you have contacted us via email or contact form and wish to have that information deleted, please reach out to us at noumanbutt252@gmail.com.

9. International Users

Nano Cropper+ can be accessed from anywhere in the world. Since all processing happens in your browser and we don't store data on servers, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your data stays on your device, regardless of your location.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. However, given our commitment to privacy-first design, we don't anticipate any major changes to our core principle: we don't collect, store, or transmit your images or personal data.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: noumanbutt252@gmail.com
  • Contact Form: Visit our contact page
