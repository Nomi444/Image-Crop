Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nano Cropper+ really free?

Yes! Nano Cropper+ is 100% free with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or subscription fees. You can use all features unlimited times without any restrictions.

Do you store my images?

No. All image processing happens entirely in your browser. Your images never get uploaded to any server, and we don't store any data whatsoever. Your privacy is 100% protected.

What image formats do you support?

Currently, we support JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats, which cover the vast majority of use cases. We're working on adding support for additional formats in future updates.

Can I use this tool on my phone?

Absolutely! Nano Cropper+ is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface automatically adapts to your device screen size.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Simply use the contact form above to send us your bug report or feature suggestion. We review all feedback carefully and prioritize improvements based on user needs.
